Onion Soup Garnished with Croutons

Description:

A classic French delight. Sweet caramelized onions in a rich, flavorful broth, topped with a generous layer of crunchy, golden-brown bread croutons.

Ingredients:

  • Onions (yellow or sweet)
  • Beef or vegetable broth
  • Butter
  • Bread (for croutons/toasting)
  • Cheese (Gruy√®re or Swiss, for topping)

Steps:

  1. Caramelize Onions: Slice the onions thinly and cook slowly in butter until deeply caramelized and soft.
  2. Add Broth:Stir in the broth and simmer for 15-20 minutes to combine flavors. Season to taste.
  3. Top with Croutons: Pour soup into oven-safe bowls. Place a slice of bread/croutons on top.
  4. Broil: Cover the bread/croutons with cheese and broil until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
