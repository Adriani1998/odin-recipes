Delicious Sourdough Homemade Bread

Description:

Artisanal perfection with a rustic charm. This homemade sourdough bread features a crispy, golden crust and a soft, airy interior with that signature tangy flavor.

Ingredients:

  • Bread Flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Sourdough Starter (active)

Steps:

  1. Mix Dough: Combine active sourdough starter, water, flour, and salt. Mix until shaggy.
  2. Bulk Fermentation: Let the dough rest, performing several sets of stretch and folds over a few hours.
  3. Shape: Gently shape the dough into a loaf and place it in a proofing basket (banneton).
  4. Cold Proof: Place the basket in the refrigerator for a cold proof (usually overnight).
  5. Bake: Score the dough, and bake in a hot oven (often in a Dutch oven) until deeply golden and cooked through.
