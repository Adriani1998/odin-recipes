Tuna and Tomato Sauce Lasagna

Description:

A comforting and savory twist on a classic. Layers of tender pasta, rich tomato sauce, and flaky tuna, baked to golden perfection.

Ingredients:

  • Pasta sheets (Lasagna)
  • Tuna (canned)
  • Tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • Onions and Garlic
  • Béchamel sauce (Milk, Butter, Flour)
  • Cheese (Mozzarella, Parmesan)

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Sauce: Sauté onions and garlic, add tuna and tomato sauce; simmer.
  2. Make Béchamel: Melt butter, whisk in flour, and gradually add milk until thickened.
  3. Assemble: Layer the baking dish with a little sauce, then pasta sheets, tuna sauce, béchamel, and cheese. Repeat the layers.
  4. Bake: Bake in the oven until the top is golden and bubbly.
